About Us

The Graduate Institute of Journalism at National Taiwan University (NTU Journalism), established in 1991, is one of the most influential academic institutions in journalism and communication in Taiwan. NTU Journalism is dedicated to cultivating professionals with critical thinking, professional competence, and a global perspective. The curriculum covers areas such as journalism theory, media practice, digital communication, and communication policy. With a strong faculty team and close collaboration with domestic and international media and academic institutions, the institute promotes academic research and industry exchange. Graduates are active in journalism, advertising, public relations, digital media, and other major fields, contributing new energy to the communication industry in Taiwan and worldwide.
